Amistead National Park

  • Located in Del Rio,Texas
  • Amistad National Recreation Area does not charge an entrance fee. User fees are charged for the following: lake use, camping, and hunting.
  • $4 for a 1-day pass. Good for 24 hours from time of purchase; they may be purchased for multiple days.
  • For current Mexico boat permit and fishing license information, visit the Conapesca website.

Guadalupe Mountains National Park

  • Located in Salt Flat,Texas
  • The entrance fee is $5.00 per person for adults 16 years of age and older. This fee is good for 7 days. Be sure to check our Fee Free days announcements via this link: Free Entrance Days in the National Parks
  • $4 for a 1-day pass. Good for 24 hours from time of purchase; they may be purchased for multiple days.
  • For current Mexico boat permit and fishing license information, visit the Conapesca website.

Big Bend National Park

  • Also located in Del Rio,Texas
  • The National Park Service operates three developed frontcountry campgrounds; Rio Grande Village Campground, Chisos Basin Campground, and Cottonwood Campground. The cost is $14 per night per site ($7 per nght with applicable pass).
  • $4 for a 1-day pass. Good for 24 hours from time of purchase; they may be purchased for multiple days.
  • For current Mexico boat permit and fishing license information, visit the Conapesca website.
